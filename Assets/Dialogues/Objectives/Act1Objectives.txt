You are the head of the student council and it is your job to find out whether the person accused has really stolen the exam scripts from the principal's office!

Press space around characters to communicate with them. By communicating with these characters, you will obtain important clues about the case. You can then enter into the principal's office to present you findings about who you think the culprit is. Make sure you are making an informed decision by analysing the information you have gathered.
